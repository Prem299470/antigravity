💡 Privacy Protection Tips

1
Google Yourself Regularly

Search your name, email, and phone number to see what's publicly visible.

2
Lock Down Social Media

Set profiles to private, disable location tagging, and limit who can see your posts.

3
Use Unique Usernames

Don't reuse the same username across platforms — it makes tracking you easier.

4
Opt Out of Data Brokers

Request removal from sites like Spokeo, WhitePages, and BeenVerified.

5
Use a VPN on Public Wi-Fi

Encrypt your traffic to prevent snooping on open networks.

6
Review App Permissions

Revoke unnecessary permissions — apps don't need access to everything.

7
Delete Unused Accounts

Old accounts with weak passwords are easy targets for hackers.

8
Enable 2FA Everywhere

Two-factor authentication blocks 99% of automated attacks on your accounts.

📡 How Live GPS Tracking Works

1
High-Accuracy GPS

Uses your device's hardware GPS sensor with enableHighAccuracy for best precision (within 3-10 meters).

2
Continuous Tracking

3
Route Trail

Records your movement path as a polyline on the map with a glowing trail effect.

4
Speed & Heading

Displays real-time speed (km/h), compass heading, altitude, and total distance traveled.

5
Privacy First
